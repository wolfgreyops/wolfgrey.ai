The Batch Creation Prompts

Run these in order. 30 minutes. Done for the week.

Educational / Tips Posts 40% of Content

Position yourself as the expert. Teach something valuable.

Create 5 educational social media posts for a [BUSINESS TYPE] targeting [AUDIENCE]. Topics I want to cover: [LIST 3-5 TOPICS] For each post: - Hook (first line that stops the scroll) - 3-4 bullet points of value - Call to action (soft, not salesy) - Relevant hashtags (3-5) Platform: [LINKEDIN / X / INSTAGRAM] Tone: [PROFESSIONAL / CASUAL / BOLD] Avoid: generic advice, overused phrases like "game-changer," motivational fluff.
Behind-the-Scenes Posts 30% of Content

Build connection. Show the human side.

Create 4 behind-the-scenes social posts for my [BUSINESS TYPE]. Things I could share about: - [A recent project or win] - [A challenge I'm working through] - [My workspace/tools/process] - [A lesson I learned recently] For each post: - Personal, authentic hook - Short story or insight (3-5 sentences) - Relatable takeaway - Question to spark engagement Don't be cringe. Don't overshare. Keep it professional but human.
Promotional Posts 20% of Content

Yes, you can sell. Just do it right.

Create 3 promotional posts for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. What it does: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION] Who it's for: [TARGET AUDIENCE] Key benefit: [MAIN VALUE PROP] Price/offer: [IF APPLICABLE] For each post: - Lead with the problem, not the product - Social proof or specific result if available - Clear but not pushy CTA - Keep under 200 words Make 3 different angles: 1. Problem-focused 2. Result-focused 3. Urgency/scarcity (if applicable)
Engagement Posts 10% of Content

Start conversations. Build community.

Create 2 engagement-focused social posts for my [BUSINESS TYPE] audience. My audience cares about: [TOPICS/INTERESTS] Create: 1. A "this or that" question related to our industry 2. An opinion prompt ("Unpopular opinion:" or "Hot take:") Rules: - Must be genuinely interesting, not forced - Should spark real debate or sharing - Keep short (under 50 words) - No "What's your favorite..." generic questions

Pro Tip: Repurpose Everything

After you create these, ask Claude: "Turn these 14 posts into 5 LinkedIn carousels and 10 tweet threads." One batch session can fuel a month of content across platforms.
